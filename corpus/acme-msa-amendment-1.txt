AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Amendment No. 1 (this "Amendment"), effective as of September 1, 2026, is
entered into by and between Globex Corporation ("Customer") and Acme Data
Services LLC ("Provider"), and amends that certain Master Services Agreement
between the parties dated January 15, 2026 (the "Agreement").

1. ANNUAL COMMITMENT. Section 3 of the Agreement is amended to increase the
Annual Commitment from $250,000 to $400,000 per contract year, reflecting the
addition of the real-time analytics services described in SOW #3.

2. PAYMENT TERMS. Invoices shall be payable within thirty (30) days of receipt,
replacing the forty-five (45) day period in Section 3 of the Agreement.

3. TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE. A new Section 4A is added: following the first
anniversary of the Amendment effective date, Customer may terminate the
Agreement for convenience on one hundred twenty (120) days written notice,
subject to payment of fees accrued through the termination date.

4. NO OTHER CHANGES. Except as expressly amended hereby, all terms of the
Agreement remain in full force and effect.
