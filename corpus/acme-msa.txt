MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of
January 15, 2026 (the "Effective Date") by and between Globex Corporation, a
Delaware corporation ("Customer"), and Acme Data Services LLC, a California
limited liability company ("Provider").

1. SERVICES. Provider shall provide the data processing services described in
one or more Statements of Work executed by the parties.

2. TERM. The initial term of this Agreement is thirty-six (36) months from the
Effective Date (the "Initial Term"). This Agreement shall automatically renew
for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives written
notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the
then-current term.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT. Customer commits to minimum fees of $250,000 per contract
year (the "Annual Commitment"). Invoices are payable within forty-five (45)
days of receipt. Provider may increase fees no more than once per contract
year; no fee increase shall exceed five percent (5%).

4. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach
upon thirty (30) days written notice if the breach remains uncured. Customer
may not terminate this Agreement for convenience during the Initial Term.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY, NEITHER
PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL EXCEED THE FEES PAID OR PAYABLE IN THE
TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM, UP TO A MAXIMUM OF $500,000.

6. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of
Delaware, without regard to its conflicts of law principles.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the
Effective Date.
